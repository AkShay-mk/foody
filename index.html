Step 2 Wipe out the bowl. Add the vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds, 1 cup of water and 1 tablespoon of salt. Flip the burgers, invert a large heatproof bowl over them and cook until medium within and the cheese is melted, about 3 minutes longer. Step 6 Mound the arugula on the bun bottoms and top with the burgers. Drain the pickled cabbage and mound some of it on the burgers; reserve the rest of the pickled cabbage for another use. Most soft drinks offer a balance between sharp bitter (or sour) flavors and sweet components, in a base of carbonated water. In the early days, the sweet syrups were balanced by neutral tasting phosphoric acid — phosphate was the secret ingredient that could balance the ph levels of these concoctions to create a more professional result. Citric acids were also popular ingredients when the addition of a citrus flavor note was appropriate to the recipe. Another popular addition sometimes used to balance sweetness was bitters, also used in popular cocktail recipes of the era.
